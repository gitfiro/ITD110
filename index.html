about us

what makes us special?

What makes us special? Avalon Café is a small family-run café located in Clarendon, Arlington, VA, that serves cupcakes and lovely Ethiopian coffee. Our founder, a first-generation immigrant from Ethiopia, was struck upon arrival to the United States by the American custom of having coffee and donuts. Preferring cupcakes, he thought – why not have Ethiopian coffee and cupcakes? And Avalon Café was born. We believe in freshness and serving high-quality products to our customers. Our cupcakes are baked from scratch with nuts and dairy products. Our ingredient includes cocoa, bourbon vanilla from Madagascar and creamy butter with fruits. We serve fresh Ethiopian coffee daily, and guests can enjoy the splendor of a full ceremony as well.
